Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new guinea pig?
Tap the "+" button on the home screen, then select "Add New Pet." Fill in your guinea pig's details and add a photo if you'd like.
How do I set up care reminders?
Navigate to the Care Schedule section, tap "Add New Reminder," and customize the timing and frequency of your reminders.
Can I track multiple guinea pigs?
Yes! GuineaPal supports multiple pet profiles, each with their own health records, care schedules, and photo galleries.
How do I use the weight tracker?
Go to your pet's profile, select "Weight Tracker," and tap the "+" button to add a new weight entry. The app will automatically create a graph of your pet's weight over time.
How does the bonding timer work?
The bonding timer helps you track socialization sessions between guinea pigs. Select the pets you want to bond, choose a location, and start the timer. You can record behaviors and make notes during the session. This helps you track progress and identify successful bonding strategies.
What is GuineaGram?
GuineaGram is our social feature where you can share photos of your guinea pigs and connect with other owners. You can post pictures, add captions, and view a timeline of your pet's growth and special moments.
How do I use the diet manager?
The diet manager helps you track your guinea pig's food preferences and maintain a balanced diet. You can record favorite foods, set feeding schedules, and get recommendations for a healthy diet. Access it from your pet's profile under "Diet Manager."
Can I set medication reminders?
Yes! In the Medical Records section, you can add medications and set reminders. The app will notify you when it's time to give medicine, schedule vet appointments, or perform health checks.
How do I use the mood tracker?
The mood tracker helps you monitor your guinea pig's behavior and emotional state. Select your pet's profile, go to "Mood Tracker," and record their daily mood, activity level, and any notable behaviors. This helps identify patterns and ensure their wellbeing.
What's in the Care Guide?
The Care Guide provides comprehensive information about guinea pig care, including housing requirements, safe foods list, common health issues, and behavior understanding. It's regularly updated with expert advice to help you provide the best care for your pets.
How do I backup my pet's data?
GuineaPal automatically saves all your pet's information locally on your device. We recommend regularly checking that your device's backup is enabled to ensure all your pet's important data is safe.
