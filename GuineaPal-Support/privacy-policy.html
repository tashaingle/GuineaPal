GuineaPal Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how GuineaPal ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application ("the App"). We are committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected and we take this responsibility seriously.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Pet Information: Details about your guinea pigs including names, photos, health records, weight data, and care schedules.
  • Media: Photos and images you choose to upload to the app.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app, including care schedules and tracking data.
  • Device Information: Basic information about your device for app functionality and troubleshooting.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • To store your pet's health and care records
  • To send notifications and reminders you've requested
  • To improve and optimize the App's performance
  • To provide customer support

Data Storage and Security

Your data is primarily stored locally on your device. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • All data is stored securely on your device using industry-standard encryption
  • Photos and media are stored securely in your device's storage
  • We do not share your personal information with third parties
  • You can delete your data at any time through the app settings

App Permissions

GuineaPal requires the following permissions to function:

  • Camera & Photo Library: To take and store photos of your pets
  • Notifications: To send care reminders and alerts
  • Storage: To save pet profiles and health records locally

Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date below.
